A note on accuracy

These are general approximate photorealistic illustrations following the BBCH scale. With 93 crops and 930 stages, some variations are inevitable. This is not a precise botanical encyclopedia — it's a practical visual tool for marketing and educational use. For most tasks, this level of detail is more than sufficient.

If you need just a few crops, contact me directly. I also welcome feedback on corrections — write to me and we'll improve this system together.
